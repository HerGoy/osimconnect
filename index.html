About OSIM

Founded to give students a voice and channel for positive action, OSIM works closely with school staff to plan extracurricular activities, charitable projects, and student welfare programs. We value collaboration, responsibility, and inclusivity.

Our Mission

  • Empower student leadership and responsibility.
  • Create platforms for student expression and skills development.
  • Serve the school and local community through organized activities.
